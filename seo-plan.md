## AIO (AI Overview) Specific Strategy

Google's AI Overviews pull from content that:

1. **Directly answers questions** — use "What is X?" headings followed by clear 40-60 word definitions
2. **Is well-structured** — H2s, H3s, lists, tables. AIO parses structured content better than walls of text
3. **Has authority signals** — reviews, backlinks, consistent citations, active GBP
4. **Is comprehensive** — covers the topic thoroughly with related subtopics
5. **Uses FAQ schema** — FAQ structured data gets prioritized for AIO extraction
6. **Is on a fast, mobile-friendly site** — technical fundamentals matter

Your best AIO opportunities right now:
- "What is behavioural daycare" — you may be the ONLY comprehensive answer on the internet
- "What is day and train" — same situation, almost no one explains this well
- "Dog daycare cost York Region" — price transparency + structured tables
- "Dog daycare [small town]" — for low-competition areas, you could be the default AIO source
