## Page 9: Spay/Neuter Policy

> Design an informational page for a dog daycare guide about spay/neuter policy. Heading: "A Note on Intact Dogs". Subtext: "Strongly recommended but not required". Body text explaining: intact males over 1 year struggle significantly in group daycare — they become territorial, mark obsessively, and fixate on females, making it difficult for them and surrounding dogs. Intact females generally do better but can still cause disruption. Recommendation: Day & Train program for intact dogs where they get individualized attention and structured management. Closing line: "We're happy to discuss the best fit for your dog — just ask." Light background. Use a callout box or highlighted section for the recommendation. Informative and helpful tone, not judgmental — we want to help owners make the right choice.
