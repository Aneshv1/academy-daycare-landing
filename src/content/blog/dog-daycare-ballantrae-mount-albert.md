## What We Offer

Academy Daycare isn't a kennel with a play area attached. We're an 8,500 sq ft facility staffed by canine behaviour specialists with 15 years of experience and a perfect safety record. Here's what that means for your dog:

### Standard Daycare — $55/day

Structured socialization for well-adjusted dogs. Your dog is placed in a temperament-matched play group — not grouped by size, but by energy level and play style. The day alternates between supervised indoor play sessions, outdoor yard time, and structured rest periods.

This isn't a free-for-all. Our staff actively manage every group, reading body language and intervening before problems start. That's why we've maintained a perfect safety record for 15 years.

### Behavioural Daycare — $95/day

For dogs that need more support — reactive dogs, nervous dogs, high-energy dogs that struggle to settle. Smaller group sizes, hands-on trainer management, and a gradual confidence-building approach. If your dog has been turned away from another daycare, or if you're worried about how they'll handle a group setting, this is the right starting point.

The most popular program. Your dog gets full obedience training — sit, down, stay, come, place command, loose leash walking — combined with daycare socialization. They receive dedicated 1-on-1 training sessions throughout the day, plus treadmill fitness and place cot training.

Think of it as a board & train program without the boarding. Your dog comes home every night, you stay involved, and results typically show within 2-3 weeks. This program is also ideal for intact dogs, who get individualized management throughout the day.

Overnight care with the same structured approach. Extended stays of 7+ nights drop to $65/night. Great for vacations, work travel, or holiday periods.
