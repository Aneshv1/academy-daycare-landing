## What Specialist Daycare Actually Means

### Temperament-Matched Groups

Most daycares sort dogs by size and hope for the best. We match by temperament, energy level, and play style. A calm Bernese Mountain Dog doesn't belong with a high-drive Belgian Malinois regardless of their weight. A nervous miniature poodle doesn't belong with a boisterous cockapoo just because they're both small.

This is especially relevant for the breeds common in Yorkville and Forest Hill — French Bulldogs, cavaliers, doodle varieties, purebred sporting dogs. These breeds have dramatically different energy levels and social styles. Grouping them thoughtfully is the difference between a good day and a stressful one.
