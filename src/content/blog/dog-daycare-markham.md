### Temperament Matching Over Size Sorting

Walk into most Markham daycares and you'll see dogs grouped by size — big room and small room. It's simple, it's fast, and it misses the point entirely. A shy, gentle golden retriever doesn't belong in the same group as a pushy, high-drive shepherd mix just because they're both 70 pounds.

At Academy, we group dogs by temperament, energy level, and play style. It takes more effort and more staff, but it means every dog in the group is actually compatible. Your dog comes home tired from good play, not stressed from surviving a bad match.

**Behavioural Daycare ($95/day)** — If your dog has been turned away from a daycare in Markham or Unionville, this is the program that exists for exactly that reason. Reactive dogs, anxious dogs, high-arousal dogs, dogs that "don't do well in groups." We run them in smaller groups with hands-on trainer management and a gradual approach to building confidence. Most facilities decline these dogs. We specialize in them.

**Day & Train ($95/day)** — This is the program Markham commuters love. Your dog gets 1-on-1 obedience training throughout the day — commands, leash manners, treadmill fitness, place cot training — with structured socialization between sessions. You drop off a dog in the morning and pick up a better-trained dog in the evening. No board and train separation, no $4,000 residential program. Just consistent daily training that compounds over weeks.

Day & Train is also our recommendation for intact dogs. Intact males over 1 year can struggle in open group play settings — Day & Train gives them the individualized management and structure they need.
