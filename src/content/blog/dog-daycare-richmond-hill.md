### Temperament-Matched Groups

Size-based grouping is the industry default because it's easy. Put all the big dogs together, all the small dogs together, call it a day. The problem is obvious to anyone who's spent time with dogs — a nervous 60-pound retriever and a dominant 60-pound bully breed are not the same dog.

We match by temperament, energy level, and play style. Every dog in the group is compatible, which means every dog is actually enjoying their day — not just surviving it.
